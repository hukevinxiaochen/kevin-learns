2 possible outcomes

```
             | Diseased (D) | Healthy (H)
-----------------------------------------
Exposed (e)  | De           | He
Not Exp (n)  | Dn           | Hn


OR = (De / He) / (Dn / Hn)
RR = (De / Ne) / (Dn / Nn) where Ne and Nn are total exposed and not exposed respectively, i.e. Ne = De + He
```
