## Recommended
### Ampicillin

2 g IV every 6 hours

### Gentamicin

2 mg per kg IV load followed by 1.5 mg per kg every 8 hours

Or 

5 mg per kg every 8 hours

## Mild Penicillin Allergy
### Cefazolin (+ Gent)

2 g IV every 8 hours

## Severe Penicillin Allergy
### Clindamycin (Solo)

900 mg IV every 8 hours

**OR**

### Vancomycin (+ Gent)

1 g every 12 hours
