## Standard Drink
12 fl oz ~ 5% alcohol beer
8-9 fl oz 7% alcohol malt liquor
5 fl oz 12% wine
1.5 fl oz 40% hard liquor

## Low risk drinking limits per week and day
14 & 4: Men
7 & 3: Women and persons > 65
0 & 3: Pregnant
