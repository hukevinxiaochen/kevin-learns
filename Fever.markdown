# Fever
[[Medical Knowledge]] > [[Sign or Symptom]]
## Triage or Emergency
One must rule out:

Sepsis
Bacteremia
Fungal Infection
Mycobacterial Infection
Parasite
Neutropenic Fever
Lymphoma
Leukemia
[[Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia]]

## Malignancy
Patients with cancer undergoing chemotherapy are at risk for a dangerous condition known as neutropenic fever.
